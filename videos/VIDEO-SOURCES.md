# Free Stock Video Resources

If you need placeholder videos or professional stock footage, here are recommended sources:

## Free Stock Video Sites:

1. **Pexels Videos** - https://www.pexels.com/videos/
   - High-quality free stock videos
   - No attribution required
   - Great for corporate/industrial content

2. **Pixabay Videos** - https://pixabay.com/videos/
   - Free videos for commercial use
   - Large selection
   - Easy download

3. **Videvo** - https://www.videvo.net/
   - Free HD stock footage
   - Motion graphics and effects
   - Some attribution required

4. **Coverr** - https://coverr.co/
   - Beautiful free stock videos
   - Perfect for website backgrounds
   - New videos added weekly

5. **Mixkit** - https://mixkit.co/free-stock-video/
   - Free video clips
   - No attribution required
   - HD quality
