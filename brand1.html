About 3TREES

Founded in 2002, SKSHU Paint Co., Ltd. (3TREES) is committed to creating a better life and healthier living environment for humanity. Listed on the Shanghai Stock Exchange A-share main board in 2016, 3TREES has grown to become a leading coating company with a brand value exceeding 33 billion yuan. With the corporate vision of "Supporting the Earth and Greening the World," 3TREES offers comprehensive coating solutions that go beyond imagination, featuring eco-friendly products and advanced technology across various applications.
