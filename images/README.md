# Image Resources Guide

This website requires the following image resources. Please place the corresponding images in the `images` folder:

## Logo (REQUIRED)
- `jinza-logo.png` - JINZA company logo (Current file included)
  - Used in navigation bar and footer
  - Recommended size: 200x200px (transparent background PNG)
  - The logo features a water droplet design with green, navy blue, and orange colors

## Product Images (Recommended size: 800x600px)
- `product1.jpg` - Emulsion Paint Series product image
- `product2.jpg` - Floor Paint Series product image
- `product3.jpg` - Waterproof Products image
- `product4.jpg` - Base Materials and Auxiliary Materials image

## About Us Images (Recommended size: 800x500px)
- `about1.jpg` - Corporate building or scenery image
- `about2.jpg` - Corporate technology innovation image

## Corporate Culture Image (Recommended size: 1000x700px)
- `culture.jpg` - Corporate culture related image

## News Images (Recommended size: 800x500px)
- `news1.jpg` - Latest news image
- `news2.jpg` - News image 2
- `news3.jpg` - News image 3

## Temporary Placeholder Images
Without actual images, the website will display colorful gradient backgrounds. You can:
1. Download free high-quality images from https://unsplash.com
2. Download free commercial images from https://www.pexels.com
3. Use your own company product photos and promotional images
4. Contact 3TREES headquarters for official product images

## Image Optimization Tips
- Use JPEG format for photo-type images
- Use PNG format for images with transparent backgrounds
- Compress images before uploading to improve website loading speed
- Recommended compression tool: https://tinypng.com

## 3TREES Brand Guidelines
As an authorized distributor, please ensure all images and content comply with 3TREES brand guidelines. Contact the brand team for official imagery and marketing materials.
