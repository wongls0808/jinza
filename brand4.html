About OYH (Oriental Yuhong)

Founded in 1995, Oriental Yuhong (OYH) is a global leader in the construction materials industry, specializing in functional building materials. With operations in over 150 countries and 30 years of pioneering industry advancements, OYH operates 68 world-class manufacturing centers. The group's flagship brand offers comprehensive waterproofing solutions spanning membranes, coatings, mortars, sealants, and waterstops, serving iconic projects worldwide from the Beijing Daxing International Airport to the World Expo China Pavilion.
